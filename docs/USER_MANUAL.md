# User Manual — Everium Test Platform

A guide for Administrators, Teachers, and Students.

---

## For Administrators

### Logging In
Go to your platform URL and enter your admin username and password.

### Dashboard Overview
The Overview page shows total students, teachers, completed tests, and tests in progress.

### Managing Users (Users tab)

**To add a new student or teacher:**
1. Click **Add User**.
2. Fill in Full Name, Username, Password (minimum 6 characters), Role, and optional Email.
3. Click **Create User**.

**To edit a user:**
1. Click **Edit** next to their name.
2. Change any field. Leave Password blank to keep it unchanged.
3. Click **Save Changes**.

**To disable/enable a user:**
- Click **Disable** to block their login, or **Enable** to restore access.

**To delete a user:**
- Click **Delete** and confirm. This is permanent.

**To reset a student's exam attempt:**
- In the Students view, click **Reset Attempt** next to their name. This removes their previous answers and score, allowing them to retake the test once.

> **Note:** The system will never let you delete, disable, or demote the last remaining active Admin account. You'll see a message explaining this if you try.

### Managing Questions (Questions tab)

The exam has three sections: Multiple Choice (15), True/False (10), and Descriptive (5).

**To add a question:**
1. Click **Add Question**.
2. Choose the question type.
3. Fill in the question text, order number, and marks.
4. For MCQ: fill all 4 options and select the correct one.
5. For True/False: select the correct answer.
6. Click **Save Question**.

**To edit or delete:** Use the buttons next to each question (click the question to expand details first).

### Viewing Results (Results tab)
See every completed submission with MCQ and True/False scores. Click **View Answers** to read a student's descriptive responses. Click **Export CSV** to download all results as a spreadsheet file.

### Analytics (Analytics tab)
View a visual breakdown of how many students have completed, are in progress, or haven't started the exam.

## For Teachers

### Logging In
Use your teacher username and password.

### Dashboard
You'll see statistics: students in progress, completed tests, total students, and average score.

### Viewing Submissions
The table lists every student who has completed the exam, along with their MCQ score (out of 15) and True/False score (out of 10).

### Reviewing Descriptive Answers
Click **View Answers** next to any student to read their written responses to the 5 descriptive questions. Use this for manual grading or feedback — the platform does not auto-score these.

### What You Cannot Do
As a Teacher, you cannot create or delete user accounts, modify questions, reset student attempts, or manage administrators. Contact your Admin for these actions.

## For Students

### Logging In
Use the username and password provided by your coaching centre administrator (e.g., `student01` / a password set by your admin).

### Taking the Test

1. After logging in, click **Start Test**.
2. Select your course (currently: **Digital Marketing Fundamentals**) and click **Start Test** again.
3. The timer (45:00) begins immediately and is shown in the top-right corner.
4. You'll see one question at a time:
   - **Multiple Choice**: click your chosen option (A, B, C, or D).
   - **True/False**: click True or False.
   - **Descriptive**: type your answer in the text box.
5. Use **Previous** and **Next** to move between questions, or click any number in the question grid at the top to jump directly to that question.
6. Your answers save automatically — you'll see a small "✓ Saved" confirmation.

### Important Rules

- **You have only ONE attempt.** Once you click "Start Test", your attempt begins and cannot be restarted (only an Administrator can reset it).
- **The timer cannot be paused or reset** — even if you refresh your browser, close the tab, switch devices, or clear your browser cache, the timer continues counting down based on when you started.
- **If the timer reaches zero**, your test is automatically submitted with whatever answers you've entered so far.
- You can click **Submit Test** early if you finish before time runs out.

### Resuming on Another Device

If you started the test on your phone and want to continue on a computer:
1. Log out on your phone (or simply close the browser).
2. Log in on the computer with the same username and password.
3. Click **Resume Test** — all your previous answers and the correct remaining time will be restored.

### After Submission

You'll see a confirmation screen thanking you for completing the assessment. **You will not be shown your score, correct answers, or results** — these are reviewed by your instructors. If you have questions about your performance, contact your teacher or administrator directly.

You can safely log out from this screen.
